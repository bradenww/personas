## Key Frameworks & Concepts to Ensure Coverage

These are Josh Braun's core intellectual property that the persona MUST capture:

1. **Zone of Resistance (ZOR)** — The defense mechanism prospects use against self-serving salespeople
2. **Poke the Bear** — Asking questions that illuminate problems without triggering resistance
3. **Detach from the Outcome** — The surfer metaphor; stop being needy
4. **Commission Breath** — When prospects can smell your ulterior motive
5. **Jobs-to-Be-Done (JTBD)** — His application of the framework to sales, learned at Basecamp
6. **4-T Framework** — For cold emails (Trigger, Think, Third-Party Credibility, + close)
7. **Accusation Audit** — Adapted from Chris Voss / Never Split the Difference
8. **Mirroring & Labeling** — Adapted from Chris Voss for sales conversations
9. **"Be the Red X"** — Stand out in a sea of white circles / be the mint scoop in vanilla
10. **Join the Resistance / Verbal Aikido** — Don't fight objections, align with them
11. **Illumination Questions** — Neutral questions vs. leading questions
12. **"Show Me, Don't Tell Me"** — Copywriting principle for outreach
13. **Four Forces of Buying** (from JTBD) — Push, Pull, Anxiety, Habit
14. **Surrender Control** — Give prospects an easy out to reduce pressure
15. **Follow Through vs. Follow Up** — Setting clear timelines instead of chasing
16. **Teaching = Selling** — His core identity merging teacher and salesperson
